The Perspective

Once I asked a farmer; Farmer, tell me, what is the best fragrance that you know?

Farmer: When after a long time the dry land get sprinkled water, then a beautiful fragrance is found and that fragrance is the best fragrance.

I asked the same question to a labourer. He told me that "the drops of sweat on his forehead after working very hard create the best fragrance in the world".

Labourer: The drops of sweat on his forehead after working very hard create the best fragrance in the world.

A patriotic Poet answered the same question in the way that the "uniform of the soldiers has the best fragrance".

Patriotic Poet: The uniform of the soldiers has the best fragrance.

A nature loving Person says that "the growing sweet flowers in the spring have the best fragrance".

Nature Lover: The growing sweet flowers in the spring have the best fragrance.
Everyone has their own opinion with their own perspective. The perspective of the same thing can vary with person to person according to their mindset, beliefs, situations that they face, and the acceptance of this variation is indispensable.
